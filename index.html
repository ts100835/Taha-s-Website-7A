Dinosaurs

Introduction to Dinosaurs

Dinosaurs are some dangerous, and some friendly reptiles who lived for 140 million years lived 65 million years ago. They evolved from Shapes and sizes from outrageous Spinosaurs
to little chick-sized Microraptors. One thing that they were good at is surviving a variety of ecosystems. Dinosaurs had straight back legs, which are perpendicular to their legs. This let them to produce less energy to move compared to other reptiles that had a irregular stance like lizards and crocodiles and others that are still alive today. Their legs were also positioned under their bodies rather than sticking out to the side to support their weight better.


The Triassic Period

At the time of the Triassic Period, all of the land and continents in the world were one big peice, instead of small divided plates like currently, called "Pangaea". The climte in the Triassic Period was mostly hot and dry, covered with large desserts. unlike today and beyond, there were no polar ice caps.
